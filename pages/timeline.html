  • How much has the use of AI had an impact on the Game Development Process? (Dissertation)

    Whilst thinking about what topic to cover for my dissertation over the summer, I thought about the impact AI was going to have on my career in the future. With us being in the modern age where artificial intelligence exists in forms such as ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini, I asked myself the question, “Could you make a game using completely generative AI?”. The answer to this question is quite complex, as there are many factors to consider, such as the current capabilities of AI, the limitations of AI, and the ethical implications of using AI in game development.

    In Summary, I'm deciding to focus my dissertation on how much AI has had an impact on the game development process so far, and how much it could have in the future. With following planned aims for this research being:

    • To investigate methods of using AI within games (Art, Production, Design etc.)
    • To consider the PLESI issues that arise when using AI in game development.
    • To investigate what complete AI usage looks like compared to normal game development
